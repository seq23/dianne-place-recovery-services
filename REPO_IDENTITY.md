# Repo Identity

## Name

Dianne's Place Recovery Services

## Purpose

Build and maintain a public recovery-housing and recovery-support website with a governed content engine, SEO/AEO/GEO infrastructure, legal disclaimers, licensure-mode controls, and protected admin surfaces.

## Current Public Positioning

The default operating mode is `pre_license`.

In this mode, the site may describe:

- recovery housing
- structured sober living
- recovery support
- community resource connection
- referral support
- services in development

The site must not claim active licensed treatment services, clinical treatment, insurance verification, medical care, therapy, medication management, accreditation, or guaranteed outcomes unless the licensure profile and validators permit it.

## Future Positioning

When licensure is active and verified, an authorized owner may switch the site to `licensed_provider` mode through the guarded licensure workflow.

## Non-Goals

- No emergency or crisis intervention service is provided by the website.
- No PHI collection is enabled in this baseline.
- No frontend-only admin password is treated as real security.
- No third-party marketing tracking is loaded before explicit consent and compliance review.
